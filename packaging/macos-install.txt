JQ Gunk - macOS VST3 Installation
===================================

1. Copy "JQ Gunk.vst3" to ~/Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/VST3/
2. Restart your DAW and scan for new plugins.

Note: This build is unsigned. On first use macOS Gatekeeper may block it.
To bypass, right-click "JQ Gunk.vst3" and choose Open, or run:

  xattr -rd com.apple.quarantine ~/Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/VST3/JQ\ Gunk.vst3
